Sacred Ceremony Preparation

Preparing for Sacred Plant Medicine Work

A comprehensive guide to preparing mind, body, and spirit for transformative entheogenic experiences.

Physical Preparation

  • Dietary Preparation:

    3-7 days before ceremony: avoid processed foods, alcohol, caffeine. Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains.

  • Medication Review:

    Discuss all medications with ceremony facilitators. Some may require discontinuation or adjustment.

  • Rest & Recovery:

    Ensure adequate sleep, reduce stress, and prepare for 1-2 days of integration time post-ceremony.

Spiritual Preparation

  • Intent Setting:

    Spend time in meditation reflecting on what you hope to learn, heal, or explore during ceremony.

  • Prayer & Meditation:

    Establish daily practice leading up to ceremony. Connect with your spiritual source.

  • Journaling:

    Begin documenting your intentions, fears, hopes, and spiritual questions before ceremony.

Microdose Circles

Gentle introduction to plant medicine with sub-perceptual doses for healing and insight.

  • Duration: 4-6 hours
  • Group Size: 8-12 people
  • Experience Level: Beginner

Healing Ceremonies

Traditional doses in sacred ceremonial setting for deep healing and spiritual exploration.

  • Duration: 6-8 hours
  • Group Size: 6-10 people
  • Experience Level: Intermediate

Vision Quests

Higher dose journeys for experienced practitioners seeking profound spiritual transformation.

  • Duration: 8-12 hours
  • Group Size: 4-6 people
  • Experience Level: Advanced

Partnership Collaboration Protocol

Partnership Principles

Reciprocal Benefit:

All partnerships must provide mutual value and support each organization's mission.

Safety First:

All collaborative work prioritizes participant safety and harm reduction.

Respect & Inclusion:

Partnerships must align with our interfaith, inclusive values and cultural sensitivity.
